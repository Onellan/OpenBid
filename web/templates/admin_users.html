Role scope guide

  • Admin can manage users, tenants, platform sources, audit history, and health across the whole deployment.
  • Portfolio manager can manage users, tenants, sources, and platform operations across tenants, but is intended for operational oversight rather than full system ownership.
  • Tenant admin can manage users and memberships only inside their current tenant workspace.
  • Analyst can work tenders, bookmarks, workflows, and queue actions inside their tenant.
  • Reviewer can review and update tender workflow inside their tenant, including operational queue actions.
  • Operator can manage day-to-day pipeline and tender processing inside their tenant, including queue retries.
  • Viewer has read-only access and cannot change workflow, queue, source, user, or tenant data.
