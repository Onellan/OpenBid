# OpenBid Brand System

## 1. Brand identity summary
**OpenBid** is a modern South African tender and procurement platform built to help businesses find, manage, track, and win tenders with confidence.  
It combines the trust and structure expected in serious procurement environments with the clarity and usability of a premium SaaS product.

**Core tagline:**  
**Open opportunities. Better bidding.**

**Positioning statement:**  
OpenBid is the modern tender management standard for businesses that need a clear, reliable, and efficient way to discover opportunities, organise submissions, and compete professionally.

## 2. Brand attributes
- Trusted
- Professional
- Transparent
- Organised
- Efficient
- Competitive
- Accessible
- Modern

## 3. Logo concept
### Primary direction
A stylised **O** that works as:
- an open portal
- a pathway into opportunity
- a structured circular frame
- subtle upward movement indicating progress, submission, and success

### Wordmark direction
- **Open** in medium weight
- **Bid** in bold
- tight, modern spacing
- app-friendly and presentation-friendly

### Monogram guidance
Use a rounded-square app icon with a deep blue field and a simplified white/teal portal-mark inside.

## 4. Colour palette
### Core
- **OpenBid Navy** `#0F4C81` — primary trust colour
- **OpenBid Blue** `#1F6FB2` — active UI colour
- **OpenBid Teal** `#12B3A8` — success/accent/action highlight
- **Canvas Mist** `#F5F8FB` — primary background
- **Slate Ink** `#1E293B` — primary text
- **Cloud Line** `#D9E2EC` — borders and dividers

### Extended neutrals
- `#FFFFFF` — surfaces
- `#EAF1F7` — muted surface
- `#64748B` — secondary text
- `#94A3B8` — tertiary text

### Status palette
- Success: `#0F9D8A`
- Warning: `#D98E04`
- Danger: `#D64545`
- Info: `#2F80ED`

## 5. Usage guidance
- Use navy for brand anchors, top-level CTAs, and navigation highlights
- Use blue for links, interactive states, charts, and selected items
- Use teal sparingly for positive motion, highlights, and success
- Keep the interface bright and spacious, with white cards on soft mist backgrounds
- Avoid using teal as the dominant interface colour

## 6. Typography
### Primary
- **Inter**
### Alternative
- **Manrope**
### Fallback
- `system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif`

### Type scale
- Display / Hero: 48–56 / 1.05 / 700
- H1: 36 / 1.15 / 700
- H2: 28 / 1.2 / 700
- H3: 22 / 1.25 / 600
- Body Large: 18 / 1.6 / 400
- Body: 16 / 1.6 / 400
- Small: 14 / 1.5 / 500
- Label / UI: 13–14 / 1.3 / 600

## 7. UI design principles
1. **Clarity first** — no clutter, obvious hierarchy
2. **Structured confidence** — every screen feels controlled and reliable
3. **Readable at speed** — important for procurement deadlines and document-heavy workflows
4. **Modern, not trendy** — polished SaaS quality without gimmicks
5. **Light theme first** — clean, enterprise-safe, and print-friendly

## 8. Iconography
- clean line icons
- slightly rounded stroke ends
- 1.75px or 2px stroke feel
- professional, minimal, dashboard-safe
- avoid decorative icons

Recommended icon categories:
- Opportunities
- Pipeline
- Documents
- Deadlines
- Compliance
- Teams
- Reports
- Alerts

## 9. Button styles
### Primary
- Navy background
- White text
- Slight shadow
- Hover transitions to brighter blue

### Secondary
- White background
- Blue border
- Blue text

### Tertiary
- Text-only or muted surface action

## 10. Form styles
- White inputs with strong border contrast
- Focus state in blue with soft glow
- Clear labels above controls
- Spacious vertical rhythm
- Validation messages concise and direct

## 11. Card and table styling
### Cards
- White surface
- 16–20px radius
- subtle shadow
- clear heading and support text
- top metadata row where useful

### Tables
- airy row height
- soft borders
- sticky headers where needed
- clear badge usage
- no overly dense enterprise grid feel

## 12. Navigation
### Sidebar / top nav
- calm white or mist background
- navy active highlight
- muted inactive items
- clear section grouping

Suggested labels:
- Dashboard
- Opportunities
- Pipeline
- Documents
- Team
- Reports
- Alerts
- Settings

## 13. Dashboard styling
Dashboard should feel like a premium operations workspace:
- KPI cards at top
- opportunity pipeline snapshot
- upcoming deadlines
- submission readiness
- compliance flags
- activity feed
- clear, readable tables and cards

### Example metric cards
- Open Opportunities
- Closing This Week
- Submission Ready
- Documents Outstanding
- Team Actions
- Success Rate

## 14. Empty states and alerts
### Empty state tone
Professional, encouraging, clear.

Examples:
- **No tenders saved yet**  
  Start building your pipeline by saving opportunities that match your business.

- **No deadlines due this week**  
  Your submission calendar is clear for now.

### Alert styling
- Info: pale blue
- Success: pale teal
- Warning: pale amber
- Error: pale red

## 15. Homepage copy
### Hero
**Find, manage, and win tenders with confidence**

OpenBid is a modern tender portal that helps businesses discover opportunities, track submissions, manage documents, and bid smarter from one central platform.

**Primary CTA:** Get Started  
**Secondary CTA:** Book a Demo

### Value proposition
OpenBid brings opportunity discovery, bid tracking, team coordination, and submission readiness into one structured platform, so you can respond faster and manage tenders with confidence.

### Features
- Tender discovery that helps you find relevant opportunities faster
- Opportunity tracking from first review to final submission
- Central document management for bid packs, certificates, and supporting files
- Alerts and reminders for deadlines, briefings, and outstanding actions
- Team collaboration across contributors, reviewers, and managers
- Reporting and insights for pipeline visibility and decision-making

### Why choose OpenBid
- Built for serious procurement work
- Clear enough for small teams
- Structured enough for enterprise workflows
- Designed for South African tendering realities
- Modern, efficient, and easy to use

### Trust section
Used by businesses that need a professional, transparent, and reliable way to manage tender opportunities and submissions.

### Final CTA
**Smarter tendering starts here**  
Bring structure, visibility, and confidence to every opportunity.

### Compact landing page version
**Find, manage, and win tenders with confidence**  
OpenBid helps your team discover opportunities, organise bid documents, track deadlines, and manage submissions from one professional platform.

## 16. Brand voice and tone

- clear
- direct
- professional
- calm under pressure
- not bureaucratic
- not pushy

## 19. Light theme guidance
Light theme is the primary experience:
- bright background
- white surfaces
- deep text contrast
- coloured accents used with restraint
- ideal for procurement and enterprise comfort

## 20. Optional dark theme
If added later:
- navy-charcoal backgrounds
- softened blue highlights
- teal reserved for positive state accents
- keep contrast high and avoid neon styling
